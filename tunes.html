Tunes

Session Class: These are the tunes that we've taught each other and played together at the Monday Session Class at the Old Library. They're a good starting point for those just beginning their journey with trad music, however these tunes are not 'just' for beginners! It's also a resource for those who'd like to learn the local repertoire. Hopefully we'll learn each other's tunes and have a lot to play when we come together. Keep an eye on the Session Class page to stay current with the community repertoire.

Friday Tunes: These tunes are a bit more diverse and cover most of what's been played at Friday Tune Time at the Irish Wolf. They're not necessarily more challenging to execute but do tend to get played at full speed with variations and ornamentation. We encourage you to make them part of your repertoire and possibly join us sometime!
