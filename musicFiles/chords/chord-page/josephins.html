This is the original key, though I've mostly heard in transposed to the key of G in sessions. On guitar, I'd suggest a capo on the 3rd fret if you're playing in F, so a D shape will be F. Otherwise you'll get tired of barring chords, plus you won't get to play with as many open strings. It's pretty straight-forward to transpose up to G, which suits an open position - just go up one step for each chord.
