This transcription came from the playing of Pete Cooper. The top line is the primary melody. Flook do a lovely version in the 'Gentle Giant' set on the album Flatfish. Visit The Session to see different versions and keys plus discussion and comments.

To play the rhythm, evenly count each 7 beats per measure. This particular piece uses the grouping of 3 2 2 with the accent on the first beat of each group (1 2 3 1 2 1 2). I learned to count this rhythm by saying words consisting of 2 and 3 syllables eg "Pineapple Mango Mango", emphasizing or accenting the first syllable of each word. I've also heard people use 'long and 'short' beats, in this case 'long short short'.
